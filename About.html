Energizing Calicut Since 1969

The Beginning

1969: Where It All Started

Our journey began in a humble auto electrical shop in Calicut. With just two dedicated hands and a big vision, we specialized in reconditioning hard rubber automotive batteries. It was a time of simple beginnings, but our commitment to quality was evident from day one.

The Golden Era

The Era of Manufacturing

As demand grew, so did we. We launched our own brand, EXCEL, employing a passionate team of 40 skilled workers. This factory was the heart of our operations, churning out durable batteries that powered vehicles across the region.

Evolution

Embracing Change

By 2000, we shifted gears towards environmental sustainability. We phased out manufacturing to focus on partnering with global giants like Exide and Amaron, ensuring our customers received the best, most eco-friendly technology available.

Present Day

Your Trusted Power Partner

Today, Excel Batteries stands as a beacon of trust in Calicut. A modern showroom, professional staff, and a legacy of over 50 years. We are more than just a shop; we are a partner in your journey.
