About Us – Powering Journeys Since 1969

For more than five decades, we have been a trusted name in the battery and power solutions industry. What started in 1969 as a small auto electrical shop in Calicut with just two dedicated workers has grown into a recognized and reliable partner for customers across the region.

In our early years, we specialized in reconditioning hard rubber automotive batteries, helping vehicle owners extend the life of their power sources at an affordable cost. As our expertise and customer base expanded, we took a bold step forward by launching our own brand, EXCEL Batteries, proudly manufactured with a team of 40 skilled workers. This milestone not only strengthened our reputation but also laid the foundation for our future growth.

By the year 2000, we chose to discontinue hard rubber battery manufacturing due to environmental concerns and changes in infrastructure. Instead of stepping back, we embraced change by becoming an authorized channel partner for renowned brands such as JC Batteries and Power Plus Batteries, while also sourcing and distributing products from several other leading manufacturers.

Today, we continue to provide dependable, long-lasting, and eco-friendly battery solutions for two-wheelers, four-wheelers, and industrial applications. Our commitment is to deliver not only quality products but also professional guidance and after-sales support that customers can trust.

To power your life with reliability, innovation, and excellence.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our customers, suppliers, and partners through integrity, service, and dedication. From our humble beginnings to becoming a leader in power solutions, our story is proof that when passion meets commitment, success follows.
