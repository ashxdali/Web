Our Story

From a Humble Shop to a Market Leader

What started as a small two-person workshop in 1969 has grown into Calicut's most trusted name in battery solutions. We didn't just sell batteries; we built relationships rooted in trust.

Today, Excel Batteries is synonymous with reliability. Whether it's your car, bike, or home inverter, we ensure you never run out of power.

Our Milestones

1969

Started as a small auto-electrical shop in Calicut with just two employees, specializing in battery reconditioning.

1980s-90s

Launched our own brand "Excel Batteries", manufacturing hard rubber batteries with a team of 40 skilled workers.

2000

Shifted focus to eco-friendly solutions. Partnered with major global brands like Exide and Amaron.

Today

Leading dealers of Amaron, Exide, Power Plus, and JC Batteries, serving thousands of customers across Kerala.
