Welcome to Our Legacy in Power Solutions

Our journey began in 1969, with a humble auto electrical shop in Calicut, run with just two dedicated hands and a big vision. In those early days, we specialized in reconditioning hard rubber automotive batteries, setting the foundation for what would later become a trusted name in the industry.

As the demand for reliable power grew, we launched our very own line of batteries under the brand EXCEL, proudly manufacturing automotive batteries with a passionate team of 40 skilled workers. This marked a turning point in our growth and reputation within the community.

By the year 2000, we decided to discontinue manufacturing hard rubber batteries due to environmental concerns and infrastructural changes in our locality. Instead of stepping back, we adapted to the evolving needs of the market by partnering with established manufacturers and sourcing top-quality batteries.

Today, we proudly serve as a channel partner of JC Batteries and Power Plus Batteries, along with offering a wide range of products from many leading brands. With over five decades of experience, we continue to provide customers with dependable, long-lasting, and eco-friendly battery solutions.

From a small workshop to a trusted power partner, our story is one of dedication, innovation, and commitment. As we move forward, our promise remains the same ‚Äî to keep powering your journey with trust, quality, and excellence.
